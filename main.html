Who was the first female to become the governor of an Indian state?

Sarojini Naidu was the first woman to become the governor of an Indian state. She governed Uttar Pradesh from 1947 to 1949.

Who among first Woman to climb Mount Everest twice?

Santosh Yadav is the first woman to climb Mount Everest twice. She is an Indian mountaineer who achieved this feat in 1992 and 1993.
